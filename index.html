About Awe-source

Awe-source is a small business dedicated to developing innovative software and hardware solutions. Our primary goal is to help people get the most out of technology and science by making life easier, not harder. We believe that technology should work for you, not the other way around.

Our solutions are designed to fit your needs, so you don't have to adapt to the tools—we make sure the tools work the way you need them to.

Our Work

We have developed a variety of products across different industries:

Pet Products:

  • Hound Halo - A one-size-fits-all invention that holds the leash up so your dog’s neck, legs, and other dogs don’t get tangled up.
  • Poopinator9000 - A doggy-doo-doo scooper that uses less plastic and makes scooping poop easier and cleaner than ever!

Vehicle Safety:

  • TailFeathers - An advanced tailgating sensor that removes guesswork and makes driving safer.
  • EVASion - Enhanced Vehicle Awareness System designed to make electric vehicles safer for everyone, especially those with hearing, visual, or cognitive challenges.

Home Automation/Monitoring:

  • Homer - An intelligent home monitoring and automation system that goes beyond the ordinary. It learns how you live and helps manage your home by monitoring water use, ensuring appliances are off when you leave, and more. "Homer, help me lower my water bills? Sure thing!"

Education:

  • Data Explorer - A data visualization tool that functions like flashcards on steroids! Teachers and students can explore complex datasets visually and intuitively, developed in collaboration with educators to improve learning experiences for visual learners.

Lifestyle:

  • Shoptimiser - Streamline your shopping trips, save time, and forget nothing. This app helps you map your items to specific shops and reorders your list based on the layout of the store.
